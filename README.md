## ABOUT DATASET
The AI & Data Science Job Market Dataset (2020–2026) is a synthetically generated dataset designed to simulate real-world hiring patterns across the artificial intelligence and data science job market. The dataset contains structured information about job roles, company characteristics, required technical skills, education levels, experience requirements, and salary ranges. It reflects hiring data across multiple countries, industries, and company sizes.

## VISUALIZATION

## INSIGHTS
Market Stability and Growth: The market showed consistent growth peaking in 2025 with 266 postings, followed by a slight normalization in 2026(213 postings). Total volume stands at 1,703 jobs with a healthy average salary of $99,646.
Hiring Urgency: Over Half of the market (51%) is characterized by Hiring Urgency, signaling a candidate-driven market where speed of recruitment is critical.
Education & Experience Equilibrium: The Demand is almost perfectly split between Bachelors (34%), Masters (33%), and PhD (33%) holders, suggesting technical competency is valued across all academic levels.
•	Senior-Level roles lead at 34.25% but the narrow margin between Entry and Mid-level indicates a sustainable pipeline for talent at all stages
Geographic And word Mode Trends: Australia (16.1%) and India (15.0) leads in posting volume by country.
Work modes are nearly equal, with Hybrid (34%) and On-site (34%) slightly outpacing Remote (32%)
Organizational Demand: startup and MNC (Multinational Corporation) sectors show the highest engagement on the radar chart, indicating these are the primary drivers of AI Data Science hiring. 

## RECOMMENDATION
For Job Seekers: Given the equal distributions of educational requirements, focus on specialized skill acquisition over higher degrees if time is a constraint. Target Australia and India for the highest volume of opportunities.
For Recruiters: With 51% High Urgency, firms must streamline their interview process. Competitive salary benchmarking should hover around the $100k Mark to remain attractive.
For Educational Institutions: Curriculum should balance both Hybrid and On-site collaboration skills, as Remote-only roles are currently the minority (32%).

## CONCLUTION
The Market is mature and diversified, showing resilience across different company sizes and global locations
